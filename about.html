About us

Owner & Master Barber : Michael Beasley


As the owner and operator of Exclusive LA in Los Angeles, California, his mission is to continually provide exceptional barbering services and sharpen the next line of future Master Barbers.

Born in Inglewood, California, Michael's passion for barbering began early in his childhood. Growing up in the salon, admiring his mothers creativity as a stylist, his journey began. For over ten years, Michael has been a family and community barber. He received his barber license from the California Board of Barbering and Cosmetology. Starting out from humble beginnings at home, to neighborhood barbershops, Michael has worked to continually hone and develop his craft throughout his career. He has now put that passion and dedication into a shop that is commited to excellence and making everyone who enters the shop, leave feeling, EXCLUSIVE.
